About BTDC

Balaji Technical Designing and Consultancy (BTDC), established in 2079 B.S., is a trusted engineering and consultancy firm providing expert property valuations, technical audits, and infrastructure solutions.

Led by qualified and experienced professionals, BTDC specializes in residential, commercial, and land valuations for banks, financial institutions, and private clients.

BTDC is committed to accuracy, reliability, and timely delivery, following industry best practices while promoting sustainable and innovative solutions.
