About BTDC

Engineering Solutions Built on Trust and Expertise

Balaji Technical Designing and Consultancy (BTDC), established in 2079 B.S., is a trusted engineering and consultancy firm dedicated to delivering precise, comprehensive, and actionable reports that empower informed decision-making.

Our Mission

To provide expert property valuations, technical audits, and infrastructure solutions with the highest standards of accuracy, reliability, and timely delivery — following industry best practices while promoting sustainable and innovative solutions.

BTDC is committed to a client-centric approach, ensuring that every engagement results in precise, comprehensive, and actionable reports that support successful project outcomes.

Our Team

Led by qualified and experienced professionals, BTDC brings together expertise in civil engineering, valuation, urban planning, and infrastructure development. Our team is certified and experienced across all the core disciplines we serve.

We maintain a robust quality assurance framework ensuring that every valuation and consultancy engagement meets the standards expected by banks, financial institutions, government bodies, and private clients.

Core Values

Our Services

Comprehensive Engineering & Consultancy Services

From property valuations to large-scale infrastructure projects, BTDC delivers expert solutions across all major engineering and consultancy domains.

🏢
Property Valuation
Expert valuations for residential, commercial, and land properties serving banks, financial institutions, and private clients across Nepal.
  • Residential property valuations
  • Commercial building assessments
  • Land and plot valuations
  • Bank and FI collateral reports
  • New valuation, revaluation & reconfirmation
🔍
Technical Audit
Comprehensive technical audits that assess the quality, compliance, and structural integrity of buildings and infrastructure.
  • Structural integrity assessments
  • Construction quality audit
  • Compliance verification
  • Defect identification and reporting
🛣️
Highway Engineering
Planning, design, and consultancy for road and highway projects with a focus on safety, durability, and sustainable construction.
  • Road alignment and design
  • Geometric design of highways
  • Pavement design consultancy
  • Road safety assessment
💧
Irrigation Systems
Design and consultancy for irrigation infrastructure supporting agricultural productivity and sustainable water resource management.
  • Irrigation canal design
  • Water resource assessment
  • Headworks and diversion structures
  • Farm irrigation planning
🚰
Water Supply & Sanitation
End-to-end consultancy for drinking water supply systems and sanitation infrastructure for communities and municipalities.
  • Water supply scheme design
  • Pipeline and distribution networks
  • Wastewater treatment planning
  • WASH project consultancy
🏙️
Urban Planning
Strategic urban planning and development consultancy that supports sustainable and well-structured community growth.
  • Land use planning
  • Urban development frameworks
  • Infrastructure layout planning
  • Municipal consultancy
📋
Insurance Surveyor Works
Expert insurance surveying services providing accurate property and loss assessments for insurance companies and policyholders.
  • Property loss assessment
  • Claim investigation support
  • Pre-insurance surveys
  • Reinstatement value reports
